Eligibility

What are the Minimum Eligibility Criteria for admission??

First thing first Candidates have Passed the Eight from any school located within the State of West Bengal are eligible for admission in Government ITIs , ITCs , JP and PPP Government ITIs or they can apply for this Government ITIs , ITCs , JP and PPP Government ITIs. This is minimum eligibility criteria.

How to apply??

Applicant is eligible for applying in any one Group of Trades (either M-Group or E-Group). Applicats found applying for both the group will be rejected. Madhymik appearing Candidates who are willing to apply online will also get Seven working days after the publication of result for filling up the online and submission of printed application form along with requisite fees to the nearest Government ITTs. You can apply from this Web-Portal : www.webscte.co.in

How the merit list will be happens for M-Groups Trades And E-Group Trades?

The Merit list of applicants for M-Group trades : On the basis of marks obtained in Madhyamik or equivalent board examination and No separate entrance test will be held.
For E-Group trades: On the basis of a Common Entrance Test (CET -2021).

Courses

What are the Trade Course for apply ??

Trade courses are divided under two groups viz. E-Group & M-Group. E-Group stands for Class Eight Passed and M-Group is stands for Madhyamik .

What are the things you have ready with you when you going to filling up the online or offline form?
(For M-Group)

  • Self attested photo copy of Admit card of Madhyamik or equivalent examination as proof of age.
  • Self attested photo copy of Mark-sheet of Madhyamik or equivalent examination .
  • Self attested photo copy of Certificate of Madhyamik or equivalent examination .
  • Self attested photo copy of Aadhar Card .
  • Self attested photo copy of Vocational pass Certificate .
  • Self attested photo copy of Certificate of SC,ST,PH Certificate(if any) A Candidate should have Certificate in his/her own name only availing admission under any reserved seat(SC/ST/PH).
  • Self attested photo copy of Kanyashree enrollee Certificate/document.
  • Two coloured passport size recent photograph (To be retained by the ITI).
  • Candidates have to produce above mentioned certificates in original for varification which is mandatory for admission in M-Group of Trades .
